ACADEMICCORE AI - PBL REVIEW 2 OVERVIEW

1. PROJECT IDENTITY & DOMAIN
----------------------------
Project Title: AcademicCore AI (Specialized LMS Assistant)
Domain: Artificial Intelligence (AI) - Specialized Chatbot
Goal: A "Syllabus Expert" providing grounded, accurate academic answers.

2. TECHNICAL ARCHITECTURE
-------------------------
Frontend: Cross-platform Flutter Mobile App.
Backend: Node.js (Express) Server.
Database: Supabase (PostgreSQL + pgvector).
AI Engine: NVIDIA AI Foundation (Mistral/Llama series) using RAG.

3. THE AI "TRAINING" LOGIC (RAG PIPELINE)
-----------------------------------------
The AI uses Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG):
- Processing: PDFs/Word docs processed at 150 DPI for efficiency.
- Retrieval: Vectors search for top 15 chunks based on context.
- Grounding: AI is forced to answer ONLY from the context (Scoring > 0.35).

4. NUMERICAL OPTIMIZATIONS & STATS
----------------------------------
- RAM Usage: Optimized to fit within 512MB (Render Free Tier).
- Heap Limit: Manually boosted to 450MB in package.json.
- Keep-Alive: 14-minute Heartbeat Cron Job for 24/7 availability.
- Search Speed: Context retrieval in < 200ms.
- Model Precision: Quiz mode presents exactly 1 question at a time.

5. KEY BREAKTHROUGHS
--------------------
- Source Chips: Clickable links reveal exactly where the AI got the answer.
- Admin Panel: Full control over Subjects with cascading DB updates.
- Selectable Text: Users can long-press to copy any part of the chat.
- Intent Detection: AI differentiates between "Question Banks" and "Modules".
